Ready to Invest? Here are some steps you can take:

- Research stocks📊: follow companies you're interested in or funds that have particularly good returns. Investing gets more fun if you are interested in what you're putting your money in.


- Save some money💵 to invest: one typically needs -- or more to start investing. Also be aware that you probably won't be able to/ or shouldn't take out your money for at least 5 years. You shouldn't invest money you'll need to pay your rent or buy groceries with. 💸


- If you are not ready to start investing yet you can always practice by pretending you have $10,000 to invest in whatever you want. Practice investing that money in different companies and funds and see what your return📉 is in 3 months, 6 months or a year.

The life of an Investor...

1. It all starts with a Piggy Bank 💰

2. Once you save up enough...you can open a Savings Account- this doesn't have such a high return rate.

3. Finally open an investment account with a higher rate of return 📈 to make more money for the future!

1. Find the bank of your choice 🏦
2. Visit the Bank Branch or Website- Remember to bring at least two forms of 🆔, this could be your school ID or Passport. ✅
3. Pick the contract 🖋 You Want.
4. Give Your Information 🔐
5. Consent to the terms 🖋
6. Put some money 💵 💵 💵 into the account!
7. Watch 👀 as your wealth multiplies.......(but not that much since the interest rate is very low in savings accounts)

* make sure your account is FDIC (Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation) Insured-- this means that if your bank goes bankrupt 🆘, the government will pay you back up to $300,000.

1. Determine which type of brokerage account best fits your needs. 🤔
2. Compare 📊 the amount of money it costs and incentives for each different account
3. Consider the different services offered for each account
4. Decide which brokerage firm you want to use.
5. Apply for the account 🖋
6. Put 💵 money 💵 into the account.
7. Start looking 👀 into different investments.
